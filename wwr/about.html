History

Our history starts with out founder and CEO Linda Ivana Rocha Vazquez. She begun rafting in 2015 with her friends and soon realized the potential of River Rafting. It started out small with just a single orange raft, but it quickly grew.

Then in 2016 she created Splash Rapids, paving the way for river rafting to be accessible to newcommers aswell as experts. She built this company from the ground up to bring excelent service to the river rafting community. Be a part of this legendary company and book your trip with us today!
