AVAILABLE TRIPS

Rabbit Rapids

White water rapids, this is for the more expirenced indviduals. This is up colorado river, and does require some hiking to get to location. Large raft is used for up to 6 people per raft.

Chocolate River

Further down on the Colorado river, these rapids are much calmer however it's no walk in the park. This is for mid to expert level expirence. No hiking required, this could require medium to large rafts from 4-6 people per raft.

Kayak Lessons

Kayak lessons take place at Dillon Reservoir. Class schedules are Saturday only from 8am-11am. Additionally you would start class with other students, where you would learn the basics and until you certified. There is limited space so please make sure to check for availability.

Horse Shoe Bend - Guided Tour

Calm and guided kayak tour through Horse Shoe Bend. With the tour guide they will guide you through and teach you about the surrounding wildlife aswell as teach you about the unique rock formations.

Grand Canyon - Guided Tour

3 day overnight guided tour, here you will camp next to the shore at a permitted camping location. Please note that camping equipment is not provided and food it not provided, but other rafting equipment is availble for rent. This tour it limited to 6-12 people.

Black Canyon - Guided Tour

This is a half day tour through Black Canyon. Perfect for beginers, this tour offers amazing view and knowledge about the surrounding spaces in the Canyon. No food is provided for this tour.
